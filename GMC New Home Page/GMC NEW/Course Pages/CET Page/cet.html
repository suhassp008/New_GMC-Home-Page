CET (Common Entrance test)
Common Entrance Test is one of the competitive exams given by students who aspire to get admission for courses like Engineering, Medical, Homeopathy, Agricultural courses, Ayurveda, Naturopathy, Unani, or Yunani, Technology (Food Technology, Dairy Technology, Food Science & Technology), Food Science, Animal Husbandry, Forestry, Home Science, Farm Science Fisheries, Pharmacy in Government Institutes. The examination is conducted by The Karnataka Examination Authority. Karnataka Common Entrance Test is mainly for Karnataka students (KCET). Each state will have different Common Entrance Tests. The test can be conducted at National level, University level or regional level. The KCET exam duration is 1 hour 20 minutes. There are 60 questions in every subject. The examination is conducted in four subjects namely Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology.

Exam pattern

Subject No. of questions
Physics 60
Chemsitry 60

Biology 60
